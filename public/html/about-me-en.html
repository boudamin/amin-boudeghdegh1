About Me

Hello! I'm Amin Boudeghdegh, born on February 2, 1990. I am a passionate and versatile professional with a strong background in electrical engineering and software development. Currently based in Gatineau, QC, I am fluent in Arabic, French, English, and Spanish, with basic knowledge of Portuguese, Italian, German, and Russian.

My journey began with a Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering, followed by a Master's degree in the same field, specializing in Electronics and Communication Systems from the University of Mohamed Seddik Benyahia in Algeria. Over the years, I have gained extensive experience working in various roles, from an Electrical Engineer to an Independent Android Developer.

In my career, I have worked with renowned companies such as Algerian Qatari Steel and Hyundai Engineering, where I honed my skills in electrical maintenance, instrumentation, and project supervision. My role as an Independent Android Developer has allowed me to explore my creativity and technical expertise, leading to the successful development and sale of several Android applications, including the sophisticated Beauty Calculator app.

Currently, I am furthering my education in computer programming at Collège LaCité, continuously expanding my knowledge and staying updated with the latest technological advancements. My technical skills span across various programming languages and software, including Java, Kotlin, C#, PHP, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Python, and more.

I am dedicated to delivering high-quality work and am always eager to take on new challenges.
